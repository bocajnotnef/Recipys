Orange Creamsicle Cocktail
==========================

Ingredients
-----------

* 1/2 oz midori
* 1/2 oz Absolut Citron
* 1/2 oz Malibu
* 1 splash sour

Instructions
------------

#. Fill a tumbler halfway with ice
#. Put ingredients in and shake vigerously
#. Fill a collins glass with ice
#. Pour drink in glass and serve

Serves: 1
